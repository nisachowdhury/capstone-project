Welcome to Accessibility Learning & Testing

Description

Screen readers and screen magnifiers are the two biggest accessibility tools as incorporated and found in the majority of OS and Windows devices. Not only this, but in recent technology there are new ways to incorporate AI to make screen reading less cumbersome.

Overview

This site is designed to bring awareness to the importance of the main accessibility features relevant to today. The site involves two case studies that involves the user to compare side by side the usability of accessibility tools versus not having them and a different range and forms to decide which are most efficient.
